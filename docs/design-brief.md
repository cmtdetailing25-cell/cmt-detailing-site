# CMT Detailing — Brand & Design Brief

## Brand Overview

**Brand name:** CMT Detailing  
**Tagline:** Mobile Detailing Done Right  
**Supporting line:** Clean. Consistent. Professional.

**Positioning:**  
Clean, consistent, professional mobile detailing with a slightly premium feel. The brand should feel trustworthy, modern, local, and quality-focused. Not flashy, not cheap, not overly luxurious.

---

## Core Pillars

| Pillar | What it means |
|---|---|
| Convenience | We come to the customer — no drop-offs, no hassle |
| Quality | Professional-grade products and technique, every time |
| Consistency | The same reliable result on every job |
| Trust | Local, honest, straightforward service |

---

## Target Customer

People who care about their vehicle, value convenience, and are willing to pay for a professional result. They're busy, they want a fair price, and they want the job done right without having to manage it themselves.

---

- **Professional** — competent and reliable, not corporate

## Design Rules

### Color
- Use **blue as the only accent color** on public-facing pages
- Do **not** use red on public-facing pages
- Red is reserved for error/validation states only (semantic UI feedback)
- Keep admin pages simple and functional — no strict color requirements there

### Layout
- Prioritize clarity and trust over flashy design
- Keep layouts mobile-friendly first
- Use clean cards, clear buttons, and consistent section spacing
- Avoid clutter, aggressive gradients, and over-designed effects

### Typography
- Strong, bold headings
- Clear, readable body text
- Section labels use small caps with wide letter-spacing

### Components
- Cards: dark background, subtle border, clean padding
- Buttons: solid accent fill for primary, bordered/ghost for secondary
- Section labels: `text-accent-light`, `text-xs`, `uppercase`, `tracking-widest`
- Bullet indicators: small filled dot in `bg-accent`

---

## Image Direction

- Focus on clean, finished results
- Sharp interior shots
- Glossy exterior shots
- Professional, controlled presentation
- Avoid emphasizing extremely dirty cars or chaotic backgrounds

---

## What This Brand Is Not

- Not flashy or aggressive
- Not discount or budget-feeling
- Not cold, corporate, or distant
- Not over-designed or cluttered
